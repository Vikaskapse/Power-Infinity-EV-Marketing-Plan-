# EV Marketing Strategy Project

## Overview
This project presents a comprehensive marketing strategy for an Electric Vehicle (EV) company targeting the Indian market.

The project includes:
- EV market analysis
- PESTLE analysis
- Porter’s Five Forces
- Customer segmentation
- Brand positioning
- Marketing mix strategy
- Pricing strategy
- Promotion strategy
- Budget allocation
- Performance metrics

---

## Key Objectives
- Achieve 5% market share by 2024
- Increase brand awareness to 15%
- Build customer loyalty
- Establish product leadership in EV innovation
